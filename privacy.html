Privacy Policy for Work Smart

Effective Date: April 14, 2026
Work Smart (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Work Smart, we may access the following information only with your permission:

  • Calendar data from Apple Calendar or Google Calendar, including:
  • Event titles
  • Dates and times
  • Event descriptions (if available)

We do not collect sensitive personal data beyond what is necessary for app functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your calendar data solely to:

  • Automatically generate your to-do list
  • Organize and display tasks based on your schedule
  • Improve your productivity experience

We do not use your data for advertising or sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your calendar data is:

  • Processed locally on your device

We do not retain data longer than necessary for app functionality.

Work Smart is designed to minimize data collection and keep calendar processing limited to what is needed for the app to function.

4. Third-Party Services

Work Smart integrates with:

  • Apple Calendar
  • Google Calendar

These services are governed by their own privacy policies.

5. User Consent

We only access your calendar after you explicitly grant permission.

You can revoke access at any time through:

  • iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Calendars
  • Your Google account permissions

6. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell your data
  • Share your data with third parties

Unless required by law.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including secure connections and best practices in data handling.

8. Children’s Privacy

Work Smart is not intended for children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected with a new “Effective Date.”
